Waki & the Winstons

Waki & the Winstons are an emerging PH based indie band known for their funky, bluesy, and OPM influence sound, featuring soulful original music and live performance. | Genre: Blues, Soul, Rock

NaÏV

NaÏV is an emerging OPM band from the Philippines known for their indie pop sound, featuring heartfelt, relatable lyrics and melodies. | Genre: Indie Pop / OPM

Off To Neverland

Off To Neverland! is an emerging genre-fluid OPM boy band from the Philippines, known for their funky, retro indie-pop sound. Under EMI Records Philippines and UMG Philippines, they recently released their single "Boombox" in April 2026. | Genre: Genre-fluid/Funk-fueled OPM.

Mint Magic

Mint Magic is a 4-piece soul, R&B, and pop group based in Manila, Philippines. They are known for their smooth, OPM vibe, with popular singles including Sukat, Kanina, Listen, and Dating Ikaw. | Genre: Soul, R&B, and Pop.

Banna Harbera

Banna Harbera was a Filipino soul and funk quintet known for their mellow, groove-driven indie music. Formed in 2013 by students at the De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde, the band became a staple of the local indie scene before officially disbanding in December 2022 after eight years of activity. | Genre: R&B, soul, and funk-influenced sound.

Lions & Acrobats

Lions & Acrobats is an independent six-piece alternative rock band from Manila, Philippines. Formed in late 2009, they are recognized for their intricate, atmospheric guitar work and unpredictable vocal melodies.| Genre: Indie Rock, Alternative Rock, Math Rock, and Post-Hardcore.

Artists

Earl Generao

Earl Generao is a Filipino singer-songwriter best known for his soulful blend of folk, pop, and R&B. Originally known for his raw acoustic sound, he has recently transitioned into a new era of his career, exploring deeper R&B and soul influences which he considers his "core inspiration".| Genre: Primarily Independent (Indie) pop/rock, acoustic, and soul, with recent forays into theater music.

Ian Quiruz

Ian Quiruz (full name Ian Felix R. Quiruz) is a Filipino singer-songwriter and musician known for his contributions to the OPM (Original Pilipino Music) scene. He is signed under the record label EvoSound. | Genre: Indie-pop, Indie-Folk

Frank Ely

Frank Ely (born Frank Cuyag) is a Filipino singer-songwriter and musician known for his fusion of folk, indie pop, and traditional Filipino kundiman and harana styles. Hailing from Cubao, Quezon City, | Genre: indie pop, folk, and romantic OPM

SHANNI

SHANNi (born Ashanti Silonga) is a rising Filipina singer-songwriter known for her unique blend of "jukebox soul," '70s-inspired torch ballads, and modern bedroom pop. Based in Marikina, she is recognized for her intimate, observational songwriting and delicate vocal delivery. | Genre: Indie Pop, Bedroom Pop, Filipino City Pop.

Earl Agustin

Earl Agustin is a rising Filipino singer-songwriter who has gained significant popularity for his retro-inspired R&B and soul sound. He is currently signed with Vicor Music and is best known for his chart-topping hit "Tibok", which went viral in 2025 after being featured in the television series Ang Mutya ng Section E. | Genre: R&B, retro soul, and funk-pop

Jopper Ril

Jopper Ril is a Filipino singer-songwriter, musician, and producer known for his modern take on the Manila Sound and classic 70s disco and R&B vibes.
